What is a DRONE?
Drone is an aircraft without any humans on board . It has a controller based system .
It is a powerful vehicle which can be lifted up in the air, fly and can be piloted remotely. 
The sizes can differ: one can be very small while the other can be as big as a human. It is also termed as a "flying robot".
Drone contains a high tech features and components such as GPS or any other navigational system .
GPS enables the drone to share its precise location with the controller itself . 
It also consists sensors which can detect the temperature and the quality of the air in which the drone is flying . 
Expenses of the drone can vary from ₹1,000 to ₹25,000 and above . A high end consumer drone can fly about 4 to 8 kms. 
Coping up with the advancing technology, drones are becoming more and more self dependent .
Its uses - Drones can be used for professional photographs , journalisms and films , rescue missions , for checking the status of terrains which are suitable for agriculture .
